AI · SaaS · Series A
NeuralPath AI
Investor Pitch Deck · 14 slides

32 slides. Investors leaving at slide 4.

The founders were engineers first. Their original deck was 32 slides of product architecture, feature lists, and technical validation — everything an engineer would want to see, nothing an investor needed to feel. By slide 4, rooms were disengaging. We started over. Not with design — with the question: what does this investor need to believe to write a cheque? From that, we rebuilt the entire narrative arc. Problem framing that made the market feel urgent. A traction slide that led with revenue trajectory, not user counts. A custom ML accuracy visualisation that translated a complex proof point into something undeniable in under 8 seconds. 32 slides became 18. Every section earned its place. The deck went on to secure meetings with tier-one investors — and one called out the accuracy visualisation as a deciding factor in their decision to proceed.

NGO · Impact Funding
BridgeLight Foundation
Donor & Grant Deck · 11 slides

Impact reports that read like government documents.

BridgeLight had the impact. Twelve thousand beneficiaries across three countries, measurable outcomes, a credible team. What they had on paper read like a government tender — dense, data-heavy, emotionally flat.

We restructured the entire deck around a single community story before introducing a single number. Built an impact pathway — vulnerability, intervention, outcome — that made the data feel inevitable. Visualised 12,000 beneficiaries as a human landscape, not a cell in a table.

A foundation director called it the clearest funding case they had seen all year. Every donor meeting that followed converted to a second conversation.

How It Works

Simple process.
Zero surprises.

01
Discovery Brief

You fill in a short brief: audience, goal, key message, existing assets. Takes 10 minutes. Saves days of back-and-forth.

02
Strategy & Structure

I map the narrative arc before touching a single slide. What does the room need to believe by the end?

03
Design & Build

First concept within 3–5 days. One direction, done properly — not three half-baked options designed to confuse you.

04
Refine & Deliver

Two revision rounds included. Final files in PowerPoint, Google Slides, and PDF. Fully editable. Always.
